JM International (PVT) LTD

JM International Private Limited was founded by Saththiyamoorthi Sajeevan on June 8, 2022.

This project is dedicated to improving the quality of life for people across Sri Lanka. Our main objective is to provide families with both essential and luxury goods at affordable prices.

We aim to offer a solution that helps individuals meet their basic and luxury needs in an easier and more efficient way within a set time frame. Through this initiative, we hope to contribute to building a better society for future generations by empowering families today.

  • Vision

    To provide high-quality products at reasonable prices while ensuring reliable delivery of goods and services to our customers.

  • Mission

    To enhance the quality of life for our customers by offering both essential goods and luxury services.
